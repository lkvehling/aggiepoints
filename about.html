About Aggie Points

How it works, how to earn, and what’s coming next.

What are Aggie Points?

Aggie Points recognize and reward UC Davis student-athletes for supporting fellow Aggies and engaging with SAAC initiatives. Check in at eligible events to earn points for you and your team, climb the leaderboard, and fuel Aggie pride.

Points Breakdown

Athletic Games

+10

Check in at UC Davis games to earn 10 points per event.

SAAC Events

+15

Attend SAAC-hosted events and check in to earn 15 points.

Quick Reference

Event Type Points How to Earn
UC Davis Athletic Games +10 Attend and check in during the event window
SAAC Events +15 Attend SAAC-hosted event and check in

Coming Soon: Challenges & Bonus Points

We’re building special challenges where you can earn bonus points for things like themed check-ins, team spirit activities, and multi-event streaks. Watch this page and the home screen for announcements.
