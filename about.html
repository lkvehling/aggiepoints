What are Aggie Points?

Aggie Points are a fun way to recognize and reward UC Davis student-athletes for showing support at athletic events and engaging with SAAC (Student-Athlete Advisory Committee) initiatives. By attending games, SAAC events, and soon-to-be challenges, athletes can earn points for themselves and their teams — climbing the leaderboard while building Aggie pride.

Points Breakdown

Athletic Games

Earn 10 points each time you check in at a UC Davis game.

SAAC Events

Earn 15 points by attending and checking in at SAAC-hosted events.

Coming Soon

Stay tuned for special challenges where you’ll have the opportunity to earn bonus points! These challenges will bring even more ways to support your fellow Aggies and compete for the top spot.
