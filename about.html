About Aggie Points

What it is, how to earn, and what’s coming next.

What are Aggie Points?

Aggie Points recognize and reward UC Davis student-athletes for supporting fellow Aggies and engaging with SAAC initiatives. Check in at eligible events to earn points for you and your team, climb the leaderboards, and build Aggie pride.

Athletic Home Games

+10

Attend a UC Davis home game and check in during the event window.

SAAC Events

+15

Participate in SAAC-hosted events and check in to earn points.

SARG Events

+15

Attend SARG events such as AAPI, AGS, and Aggie Mentor programs to earn points.

Points Scale — Quick Reference

Event Type Points How to Earn
UC Davis Athletic Games +10 Attend and check in during the event window
SAAC Events +15 Attend SAAC-hosted event and check in
SARG Events (AAPI, AGS, Aggie Mentor, etc.) +15 Attend qualifying SARG event and check in
