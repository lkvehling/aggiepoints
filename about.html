About Aggie Points

Aggie Points is a student engagement and reward system that encourages attendance at UC Davis events, from athletics to cultural activities. By checking in at events, you earn points for yourself and your team.

SAAC Events

SAAC (Student-Athlete Advisory Committee) events are worth +10 points each.

SARG Events

SARG events include AAPI, AGS, Aggie Mentor, and other student resource group events. Attending these events earns you +15 points.

Get Involved

Explore upcoming events on the Events page, check in to participate, and track your progress on the Leaderboard. Every check-in helps your team climb the rankings!
